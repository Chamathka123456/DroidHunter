# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, disability, gender identity, sexual orientation, race, religion, or other personal characteristics.

## Expected Behavior
- Be considerate and respectful.
- Refrain from demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behavior.
- Provide constructive feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, offensive comments, or personal attacks.
- Threats or intimidation.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Reporting
If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please contact: **chamath123pr@gmail.com **

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove or block users who violate the Code of Conduct.
